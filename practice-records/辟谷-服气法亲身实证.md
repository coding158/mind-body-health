## 二、亲身经历 · The Firsthand Experience

我做过的纯清水辟谷：**7天、14天、28天**各有经历。
每一次，**复谷期与辟谷期等长**——辟7天就复7天，辟28天就复28天。

During these fasts: **plain water only — no electrolytes, no other beverages.**
For each fast, the refeeding period was **as long as the fast itself** — 7 days out for 7 days in, 28 out for 28 in.

辟谷当天的状态：**没有饥饿感，能正常上班、正常运动。**
这不是靠意志硬扛，而是靠下面这套服气方法，让肠胃机制整个安静下来。

State during fasting days: **no hunger, able to work and exercise normally.**
Not by willpower against hunger, but by the breath method below, which quiets the entire digestive mechanism.

### 一个帮助理解的类比 · An Analogy

有点类似某些哺乳动物的冬眠——让肠胃的运作机制**主动停下来**，而不是一边运转一边忍饥。这个机理，在没有亲身实践过之前，确实很难单凭推理证明。

Somewhat like hibernation in certain mammals — the digestive mechanism is brought to a halt rather than left running while hunger is endured. This mechanism is genuinely hard to prove by reasoning alone, without firsthand practice.

## 三、服气方法（仅作记录，非指南）· The Breath Method (recorded, not instructed)

> 以下仅为如实记录我在老师当面指导下所行的方法，**不构成操作教学**。

- **时辰**：每天早晨 7–9 点，胃经当令之时
- **方法**：以"拉风箱式"的服气方式吞气（理论源自干昌新所述的老子服气法）
- **次数**：吞气约 49 次，直到产生"打饱嗝"的饱足感为止
- **效果**：当天即无饥饿感，可正常工作与运动
- **铁律**：全程**只喝清水**，不喝任何其他饮料，包括电解质水

- **Time**: each morning 7–9 a.m., the hours when the Stomach meridian is ascendant
- **Method**: "bellows-style" breath-swallowing (theory drawn from Laozi's qi-swallowing method as described by Gan Changxin)
- **Count**: roughly 49 swallows, until a burp-like sense of fullness arises
- **Effect**: no hunger that day; able to work and exercise normally
- **Iron rule**: **plain water only** throughout — no other beverages, including electrolyte water

## 四、⚠️ 必备前提——缺一不可 · Prerequisites — None Optional

**这套方法的安全性，完全锁在以下前提里。把结果（"清水也能28天"）单独拿走、丢掉前提，剩下的就是会伤人的东西。**

The safety of this method is entirely locked inside the following prerequisites. Take the result ("28 days on water alone") and strip the prerequisites, and what remains can hurt people.

1. **当地有经验老师的当面指导**
   服气的手法、节奏、身体反应的判读，靠文字学不会，必须有人当面带。
   *In-person guidance from an experienced local teacher.* The technique, rhythm, and reading of bodily responses cannot be learned from text.

2. **长期服气训练打底**
   服气能让肠胃停下来，是长期训练的结果，不是临时能用的技巧。
   *A foundation of long-term breath training.* The ability to quiet the gut is built over time, not a trick to deploy on demand.

3. **循序渐进，多年累积**
   28天不是冲出来的，是从短到长、慢慢走、不断听身体多年走出来的。**不可越级。**
   *Gradual progression over years.* The 28-day fast was not "pushed to"; it was reached slowly, from short to long, listening to the body over years. **No skipping levels.**

4. **等长复谷，流食过渡**
   复谷期与辟谷期等长，从流食和极清淡食物开始，耐心恢复到正常。
   *A refeed equal in length to the fast,* beginning with liquids and very light food.

## 五、⚠️ 给所有人的危险警示 · Danger Warnings for Everyone

- **无指导、无训练者：切勿模仿。** 没有上述前提，长时间辟谷（尤其不补电解质）可能危及生命。
  *Without guidance or training: do not imitate.* Extended fasting (especially without electrolytes) can be life-threatening absent the prerequisites above.

- **"只喝清水"是这套特定方法的一部分，不是通则。**
  现代断食的主流安全做法是补充电解质。本文的清水做法依赖服气训练支撑，**不可随意套用到普通断食上。** 普通人贸然停掉电解质，极度危险。
  *"Plain water only" is part of this specific method, not a general rule.* Mainstream safe fasting supplements electrolytes. Do not transplant the plain-water approach onto ordinary fasting.

- **复食综合征（refeeding syndrome）是真实且可致命的。** 长时间辟谷后的复谷，比辟谷本身更需要谨慎。
  *Refeeding syndrome is real and can be fatal.* Coming off a long fast demands more care than the fast itself.

- **身体发出任何异常信号——心悸、头晕、虚弱、不对劲——立即停止，必要时就医。** 提前结束从来不是失败。数字只是数字，身体不是。
  *Any abnormal signal — palpitations, faintness, weakness, wrongness — stop immediately and seek medical care if needed.* Ending early is never a failure.

- **有基础疾病、孕期、未成年人、体重过低者：不适用本文任何内容。**
  *Those with underlying conditions, pregnant, minors, or underweight: none of this applies to you.*

This is the project's consistent measure: **point to the direction of the path, but never force a method on someone without the prerequisites.**
