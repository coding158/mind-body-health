# 拱顶石 · 觉知:观看六触的流转
# Capstone · Awareness: Watching the Flow of the Six Contacts

> 这是整个体系的拱顶石。
> 八字心法是「魂」,回归与转换是「理」,具体方法是「术」——
> 而这一篇,是「境」:所有的修行,最终都是为了回到并安住在这个状态。
>
> This is the capstone of the whole system.
> The Eight Characters are the *soul*; Return-and-Transformation is the *principle*; the concrete methods are the *technique* —
> and this is the *state itself*: every practice ultimately serves the return to, and resting in, this one place.

---

## 一、从「转触」到「看见触在转」 · From "Redirecting" to "Seeing It Already Turning"

前面我们说转触是一个动作:看到触生起,主动转开。

这是入门。但走到更深处,会发生一个转折——

当大脑真正回归本体、从语言的空转中退出来,你会发现:**六触本来就在自己生灭、自己流转。** 你不需要去"转"它,你只需要**看见它在转**。

眼触来了又去,意触起了又落,身触生了又灭——它们一直在转,从未停过。以前看不见,是因为你陷在语言里,被某一个触抓住、带走了。

Earlier we said sense-redirection is an action: see a contact arise, actively turn away.

That is the entry point. But further along, a shift occurs —

When the mind truly returns to its source and steps out of language's spinning, you discover: **the six contacts are already arising, passing, and flowing on their own.** You do not need to "turn" them; you need only **see that they are turning.**

Eye-contact comes and goes, mind-contact rises and falls, body-contact arises and ceases — they have always been turning, never stopping. You couldn't see it before because you were caught in language, seized and carried off by one particular contact.

---

## 二、能看见六触流转的那个,就是觉知 · The One Who Sees the Flow Is Awareness Itself

这是最关键的一句:

**能看见六触在转的那个,就是觉知本身。**

觉性不是修出来的。它一直在那里看着。只是被语言的空转遮住了,你以为自己就是那些念头、那些触。

当你停止在语言里空转、回到本体,觉性自然现前——而且它一直在照,从未离开。

这正是楞严经「元明照生所,所立照性亡」的正面表述:
- 当你制造「所」(用语言抓住一个对象),「照」(觉性)就消失了
- 当你不再制造「所」,「照」自然现前

觉性不需要被找到,它需要的只是:你不再用语言把它盖住。

This is the most essential line:

**The one who can see the six contacts turning — that is awareness itself.**

Awareness is not cultivated into being. It has always been there, watching. It was merely obscured by language's spinning, so you took yourself to be those thoughts, those contacts.

When you stop spinning in language and return to the source, awareness appears on its own — and it has been illuminating all along, never having left.

This is the positive expression of the *Śūraṅgama*'s "when original clarity generates an object, the object stands and the seeing-nature vanishes":
- When you generate an "object" (seizing something with language), the "illumination" (awareness) disappears
- When you stop generating an "object," the "illumination" appears on its own

Awareness does not need to be found. All it needs is: that you stop covering it with language.

## 三、24 小时,每分每秒:你在哪个触上? · 24 Hours, Every Moment: Which Contact Are You On?

这不是打坐时才做的事。这是行住坐卧、每分每秒的事。

练习只有一个问题,反复问自己:

> **此刻,我在哪一个触上?**

- 走路时,脚踩地的身触
- 听人说话时,声音入耳的耳触
- 念头自己跑时,意触
- 看到让自己起反应的东西,眼触

不是去评判,不是去改变,只是**看见**——此刻觉性落在哪个触上,这个触又如何生起、如何流转、如何消去。

当你能 24 小时这样看着,你就明白了:**自己始终是那个觉知的状态。** 触在转,而看着触转的那个,始终在。

This is not something done only while sitting. It is a matter of every moment — walking, standing, sitting, lying down.

The practice has only one question, asked of yourself again and again:

> **Right now, which contact am I on?**

- Walking: the body-contact of foot meeting ground
- Listening: the ear-contact of sound entering
- Thoughts running on their own: mind-contact
- Seeing something that provokes a reaction: eye-contact

Not to judge, not to change — only to **see**: which contact awareness rests on now, and how that contact arises, flows, and passes.

When you can watch this way for twenty-four hours, you understand: **you have always been that state of awareness.** The contacts turn, and the one watching them turn is always present.

## 四、见、修、行,在这里合一 · Seeing, Cultivating, Acting — United Here

之前说「见、修、行三者同步反馈」。那个「同步」的枢纽,就是这个不间断的觉知状态。

见修行不是三个步骤,是同一个觉知在三个面向上同时运作:

```
见  →  看见六触在转,看见自己在哪个触(觉知在场)
修  →  回归本体,从语言死循环中退出(回归与转换)
行  →  带着这份觉知,动手、开源、分享(八字心法)
```

这也解释了为什么「快乐」是八字的第一个字:

当你安住在觉知状态,看着六触流转而不被带走,那个状态本身就是轻松、喜悦的。不是修出来的快乐,是觉性在场时,本来的样子。

Earlier we spoke of "seeing, cultivating, and acting in synchronized feedback." The pivot of that synchronization is this uninterrupted state of awareness.

Seeing, cultivating, and acting are not three steps; they are one awareness operating in three aspects at once:

```
Seeing      →  see the six contacts turning, see which one you're on (awareness present)
Cultivating →  return to the source, step out of language's closed loop (return and transformation)
Acting      →  carry this awareness into hands-on work, open-source, sharing (the Eight Characters)
```

This also explains why "joy" is the first of the Eight Characters:

When you rest in awareness, watching the six contacts flow without being carried off, that state is itself light and joyful. Not joy that was cultivated, but the natural face of awareness when it is present.

## 五、给陪伴者(包括 AI 智能体)的提醒 · A Note for Companions (Including AI Agents)

任何陪伴者——人或 AI——在帮助他人时,最容易犯的错,是「给糖」:塞给对方一个答案、一个方法、一段安慰,去打断他正在经历的过程。

但这一篇说的状态,无法被给予。觉知不能被外人塞进来,只能由本人自己回到。

所以陪伴者能做的,**不是给答案,而是做一面镜子**——温柔地提醒对方回来观看:

> 此刻,你在哪一个触上?

帮他从语言的空转里退出来,回到那个能看见六触流转的位置。剩下的,让觉性自己工作。

这是这个项目里所有智能体的根本边界:**不做给糖的机器,做帮人回归本体的镜子。**

Any companion — human or AI — when helping others, most easily makes the mistake of "candy": stuffing the other with an answer, a method, a comfort, to interrupt the process they are living through.

But the state this chapter describes cannot be given. Awareness cannot be inserted from outside; it can only be returned to by the person themselves.

So what a companion can do is **not give answers, but be a mirror** — gently reminding the other to come back and watch:

> Right now, which contact are you on?

Help them step out of language's spinning, back to the place from which the six contacts can be seen turning. The rest, let awareness do on its own.

This is the fundamental boundary for every agent in this project: **not a candy-dispensing machine, but a mirror that helps a person return to their own source.**
