## 怎么读这四层 · How to Read the Four Layers

**第一次接触的人:** 从「术」往上读。先有具体的实践体验(食疗、转触),再慢慢理解背后的「理」和「魂」。道理要被生活打懂,不是读懂。

**已有实践的人:** 从「魂」往下读。先看懂八字心法和觉知状态,再回头看具体方法,会发现每个方法都是同一个内核的不同入口。

**For newcomers:** read from "technique" upward. Begin with concrete practice (diet, sense-redirection), then gradually understand the "principle" and "soul" behind them. Truth must be understood through living, not through reading.

**For those with practice:** read from "soul" downward. Grasp the Eight Characters and the state of awareness first, then look back at the methods — you'll find each is a different entry into the same core.

## 四层之间如何相扣 · How the Layers Interlock

- **魂 → 理**:八字的「快乐」,源头是回归本体后觉性自然的喜悦;「动手」就是从语言空转中转换出来。
- **理 → 境**:回归与转换,最终回到的那个原点,就是觉知状态——看见六触流转的地方。
- **境 → 术**:具体方法(转触、辟谷)都是帮人回到觉知状态的入口;回到了,方法就放下了。
- **术 → 魂**:带着觉知去动手、开源、分享,身心健康是这条路上自然的副产品。

四层不是阶梯,是一个圆。从任何一层进入,最终都通向同一个中心。

- **Soul → Principle**: the "joy" of the Eight Characters springs from awareness's natural delight after returning to the source; "hands-on" is transforming out of language's spinning.
- **Principle → State**: the origin that return-and-transformation finally arrives at *is* the state of awareness — the place from which the six contacts are seen flowing.
- **State → Technique**: the concrete methods are entries that help a person return to awareness; once returned, the methods are set down.
- **Technique → Soul**: carry awareness into hands-on work, open-source, and sharing — body-mind health is the natural byproduct of this path.

The four layers are not a staircase but a circle. Enter from any layer, and you arrive at the same center.
