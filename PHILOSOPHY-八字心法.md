# 项目总纲 · 八字心法

# Core Philosophy · The Eight Characters

> 快乐 · 动手 · 开源 · 分享
> Joy · Hands-On · Open-Source · Sharing

> 这八个字是整个项目的魂。
> 后面所有的方法（转触、辟谷、实践记录）都是为这八个字服务的。
> 
> These eight characters are the soul of this project.
> Every method that follows (sense-redirection, fasting, practice records) serves them.

-----

## 为什么是这八个字 · Why These Eight

这八个字不是口号，每一个都是**对治**——针对一个具体的执著或困境而立。
理解它们，需要丰富的经历和照见本性的智慧。

These are not slogans. Each one is a **remedy** — established against a specific attachment or trap.
To understand them truly takes lived experience and the wisdom of seeing one’s own nature.

-----

## 一、快乐 · Joy

**对治：把静心当成苦修、压制、与自己对抗**
**Remedy for: treating practice as self-punishment, suppression, fighting oneself**

很多人把修行变成了折磨自己的工具——拼命”降伏内心”，痛苦没有按时消失就责备自己。这是第二重痛苦。

真正的静心不是逼出来的。当你看见一个触从哪里生起，不压制、不跟随，轻轻转开，觉性自然回来——那个回来的状态本身就是快乐。这个快乐不是修来的，是遮蔽少了之后本来就在的。

所以用”快乐”这个词，是要提醒：如果你的修行让你越来越苦、越来越紧，那方向就错了。

So many turn practice into a weapon against themselves — straining to “subdue the mind,” then blaming themselves when the pain doesn’t lift on schedule. That self-judgment is a second suffering.

Real stillness is not forced. When you see where a sense-contact arises, and neither suppress nor follow it but gently turn — clarity returns on its own. That returning *is* joy. Not joy you cultivated, but joy that was always there once the obscuration thinned.

The word “joy” is here as a warning: if your practice makes you tighter and more miserable, the direction is wrong.

-----

## 二、动手 · Hands-On

**对治：停留在动大脑、空想、语言的反复空转**
**Remedy for: staying in the head — overthinking, abstraction, language spinning in circles**

痛苦的根，往往是语言在大脑里停不下来。关于一件事的念头反复出现，越想越紧，越想越深。而你用来对治它的——更多的思考、更多的道理——又是更多的语言，投进同一个空转的系统。

奥修和托利都指向同一件事：头脑停下来的那个间隙，才是静心，那里没有语言。

“动手”就是从语言里走出来，落到真实的身体和行动里。做饭、敲胆经、走路、写一行真实的实践记录、提交一次代码——这些都是把人从大脑的空转里拉回到当下。

The root of suffering is often language that won’t stop in the head. Thoughts about one thing repeat, tighten, deepen. And the tool you reach for to fix it — more thinking, more doctrine — is just more language poured into the same spinning system.

Osho and Tolle point at the same thing: the gap where the mind goes quiet, that is stillness, and there is no language there.

“Hands-on” means stepping out of language and landing in the real body and real action. Cooking, tapping the meridians, walking, writing one honest practice note, committing one piece of code — each pulls a person out of the mind’s spinning and back into the present.

-----

## 三、开源 · Open-Source

**对治：封闭自己，把所知所得藏起来**
**Remedy for: closing oneself off, hoarding what one knows and has gained**

人有一种本能，把自己摸索出来的东西藏起来——怕被超越，怕不再特别，怕付出没有回报。这种封闭本身就是一种执著，也让自己停在原地。

开源是把这种封闭打开。把走过的路、包括走偏的弯路，都摊开来给人看。这不只是技术意义上的开源代码，更是把自己向他人、向世界打开的姿态。

奇妙的是,一旦打开,自己反而走得更远——因为别人的反馈,会照见你自己看不见的盲点。

People instinctively hide what they’ve worked out — afraid of being surpassed, of no longer being special, of giving without return. That closing-off is itself an attachment, and it keeps you stuck where you are.

Open-source pries that closure open. You lay out the whole path — including the wrong turns — for everyone to see. This is not just open-source code in the technical sense; it is a posture of opening oneself toward others and toward the world.

The strange thing is: once you open, you go further yourself — because others’ feedback reveals the blind spots you couldn’t see.

-----

## 四、分享 · Sharing

**对治：舍不得自己珍爱的东西或感情**
**Remedy for: clinging to what one treasures — possessions or feelings**

这是最深的一个。开源还只是分享知识和方法，分享则触及更难放手的东西——你最珍爱的东西，你最深的感情。

之前讨论过”爱 vs 执著”。舍不得分享自己珍爱的,本质就是 clinging——把”我的”抓得死死的。而分享,是把那个攥紧的手松开。

不是要你失去所爱,而是发现:真正的爱不是占有和收紧,是流动、给出、允许它如其所是。你越是分享,越会发现珍爱的东西没有变少——反而因为流动而活了起来。

This is the deepest one. Open-source still only shares knowledge and method; sharing reaches what is far harder to release — what you treasure most, your deepest feelings.

We discussed “love vs. clinging” earlier. The reluctance to share what you treasure is, at root, clinging — gripping “mine” with a closed fist. Sharing is opening that fist.

It does not ask you to lose what you love. It reveals that real love is not possession and contraction, but flow, giving, allowing things to be as they are. The more you share, the more you find that what you treasure has not diminished — it has come alive through flowing.

## 八字之间的关系 · How They Connect

```
快乐  是方向    —— 修行若不通向快乐，方向就错了
动手  是路径    —— 从语言空转中出来，落到真实
开源  是姿态    —— 向他人和世界打开
分享  是放手    —— 松开最难松开的执著

Joy       is the direction  — if practice doesn't lead to joy, the direction is wrong
Hands-On  is the path       — out of language-spinning, into the real
Open      is the posture    — opening toward others and the world
Sharing   is the release    — letting go of the hardest attachment
```

四个字层层递进，从对自己（快乐、动手），到对他人和世界（开源、分享）。
身心健康不是终点,是走在这条路上自然的副产品。

The four move outward in stages — from oneself (joy, hands-on) to others and the world (open, sharing).
Body-mind health is not the destination; it is the natural byproduct of walking this path.
